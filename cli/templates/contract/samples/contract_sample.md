## 2. Term

This Agreement shall commence on **March 1, 2026** and continue through **February 28, 2027**, with automatic renewal for successive one-year terms unless either party provides ninety (90) days written notice of non-renewal.

Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information shared during the term of this Agreement and for two (2) years thereafter.

## 5. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement with thirty (30) days written notice. Upon termination, Provider shall be paid for all services rendered through the effective date of termination.
